Who Are We

Welcome to our church community, where we strive to follow the teachings of Christ based on the Bible.

Our Beliefs

  • We believe in the Bible as the inspired and infallible Word of God.
  • We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, death on the cross to provide atonement for sin, bodily resurrection, and ascension to heaven.
  • We believe in the Holy Spirit, who convicts of sin, leads to repentance, and empowers believers for Christian living and service.
  • We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not by works.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples, and love and serve our community.

Our Values

  • Love: We strive to love God and love others as Christ loved us.
  • Truth: We are committed to teaching and upholding Biblical truth.
  • Service: We serve one another and our community with humility and compassion.
  • Fellowship: We value fellowship and support among believers.
  • Mission: We are passionate about fulfilling the Great Commission.
